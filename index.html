Our Robots

Unitree H1 Humanoid Robot

The Unitree H1 is a universal humanoid robot standing 1.8m tall with 19 degrees of freedom, capable of walking at up to 3.3 m/s. Ideal for research, industrial automation, and service robotics in Ireland and the UK.

Unitree H2 Humanoid Robot

The Unitree H2 is the next-generation humanoid robot featuring advanced dexterous hands and a 40kg payload capacity. Designed for demanding industrial tasks and advanced research.

Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot

The Unitree G1 is the most affordable humanoid robot available, featuring 43 degrees of freedom and dexterous hands. Perfect for AI research, universities and technology companies.

Unitree Go2 Robot Dog

The Unitree Go2 is an intelligent quadruped robot dog with advanced terrain adaptability and 4D LIDAR. Ideal for inspection, research, and education applications.

Unitree B2 Robot Dog

The Unitree B2 is an industrial-grade quadruped robot built for heavy-duty tasks, with a 40kg payload and designed to operate in harsh outdoor environments.

Unitree B2-W Wheeled Robot

The Unitree B2-W combines legged and wheeled locomotion for versatile industrial use, capable of navigating varied terrain at speed.

Unitree R1 Humanoid Robot

The Unitree R1 is a wheeled humanoid robot designed for service applications, featuring expressive interaction and strong payload capabilities.
